🏗️ About Us

RK Pipes & Fittings, proudly operating as RK Builds Mart, stands as a top-tier wholesale agency with an impressive 25+ years of unwavering experience. We are profoundly dedicated to supplying high-grade building materials across the entirety of Tamil Nadu, establishing ourselves as an indispensable cornerstone in the dynamic construction industry. Our commitment extends beyond mere supply; we aim to foster growth and enable success for all our partners.

Our robust and strategically developed logistical network is a key differentiator that truly sets us apart. This extensive network empowers us to efficiently serve over 500 outlets within the vibrant urban landscape of Chennai and an even more expansive network of more than 1000 outlets throughout the entire state. This unparalleled reach ensures that timely and consistent material delivery is a guarantee, regardless of the project's scale or geographical location, thereby minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity for our clients.

We are widely recognized and highly respected for our unwavering reliability, exceptional speed in delivery, and highly competitive pricing strategies. These core values have been instrumental in cultivating lasting and strong relationships with a diverse and esteemed clientele, including major contractors, discerning retailers, and leading real estate developers. From supporting the foundational needs of mega infrastructure projects to supplying essential materials for intricate residential developments, RK Builds Mart is honored to be the steadfast backbone of construction material supply. Our unwavering commitment to excellence is further exemplified by our in-house manufactured pipes and tanks, which have consistently earned industry-wide recognition for their superior strength, remarkable durability, and uncompromising overall quality. We continuously strive to innovate and provide cutting-edge solutions that proactively meet and exceed the evolving demands of the modern construction sector.
